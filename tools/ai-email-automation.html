Tool Breakdown

1. Brevo

Best for: UK SMEs that want low entry cost and multi-channel campaigns from one platform.

Highlights: pay-by-email-volume model, automation workflows, AI content support, SMS/WhatsApp options.

Affiliate angle: Brevo runs a content-focused affiliate program with cookie tracking and PartnerStack management.

2. GetResponse

Best for: businesses wanting email automation plus landing pages and conversion funnels in one tool.

Highlights: strong automation builder, list growth tools, and AI-driven campaign support.

Affiliate angle: tiered recurring commissions with a long cookie window via PartnerStack.

3. MailerLite

Best for: owners who need fast onboarding and straightforward automation without heavy complexity.

Highlights: clean UI, unlimited emails on paid tiers, useful templates and automation basics.

Affiliate angle: recurring commission model suitable for review and comparison content sites.

4. Omnisend

Best for: ecommerce stores using Shopify/WooCommerce that need lifecycle automations and channels beyond email.

Highlights: strong ecommerce automation workflows, push and SMS support, and ecommerce-oriented reporting.

Affiliate angle: recurring commissions through Impact with a defined attribution window.

UK Pricing Notes

  • Check whether checkout is in GBP, USD, or EUR before subscribing.
  • Confirm VAT treatment for UK billing entities.
  • Compare monthly vs annual discounts; headline prices are often annual-billing rates.
  • For list-based pricing, costs can rise quickly as contacts grow.

Who Each Tool Is Best For

  • Brevo: budget-sensitive SMEs needing email plus SMS in one dashboard.
  • GetResponse: teams that want email automation tied to lead capture and conversion funnels.
  • MailerLite: service businesses and creators prioritising ease of use.
  • Omnisend: ecommerce-first businesses needing stronger post-purchase and cart recovery flows.

Pros & Cons of AI Email Automation for SMEs

Pros

  • Reduces manual campaign work and follow-up admin.
  • Improves response timing and consistency.
  • Can increase repeat revenue with lifecycle automations.

Cons

  • Pricing can scale quickly with contact growth.
  • Poor setup can lead to irrelevant automation and lower engagement.
  • Some advanced AI and reporting features are gated to higher tiers.

FAQ

What is the best AI email automation tool for UK small businesses?

It depends on your model: Brevo for cost control, GetResponse for all-in-one workflows, MailerLite for usability, and Omnisend for ecommerce automation depth.

Do these tools support affiliate monetisation opportunities?

How should I choose my first tool?

Start with your biggest revenue bottleneck, then run one workflow first (welcome, lead nurture, or abandoned cart) before expanding into complex journeys.
