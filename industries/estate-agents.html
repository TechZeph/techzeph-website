Tool Breakdown

HubSpot

Strong for agencies wanting one platform for lead capture, tasking, and marketing workflows. Includes broad integrations and training resources.

Pipedrive

Excellent for agencies focused on viewing every valuation and offer stage clearly. AI sales assistant features support prioritization.

Brevo

Good value for nurture campaigns via email and SMS with automation flows useful for buyer/seller journeys.

Tidio

Useful for on-site chat response and lead qualification before human handoff, especially outside business hours.

UK Law and Data Protection Fit

  • HubSpot, Pipedrive, Brevo, and Tidio publish GDPR-oriented documentation and data-processing terms.
  • For UK estate data, configure lawful basis, consent controls, and retention schedules before launch.
  • Use suppression rules and PECR-compliant opt-out handling for nurture automation.

Not legal advice. Check UK GDPR and PECR obligations for your agency model and marketing process.

Who Each Tool Is Best For

  • HubSpot: agencies wanting one system for CRM plus marketing.
  • Pipedrive: sales-led teams with strong pipeline discipline.
  • Brevo: teams prioritizing affordable automated outreach.
  • Tidio: agencies that rely on website-originated leads.

Conclusion

For UK estate agents, start with HubSpot or Pipedrive for pipeline control, then add Brevo or Tidio based on nurture and chat needs.
