## Quick Verdict

FieldPulse is expanding its field‑service platform with **Field Intelligence**, a suite of AI tools. Operator AI acts as a 24/7 dispatcher, capturing every phone call and booking jobs with natural conversation. Chat AI converts website visitors into booked jobs with branding‑aligned chat responses. However, pricing is available only via custom quote, and the system may not yet support UK‑specific forms.

## What This Tool Does

FieldPulse is an all‑in‑one job management and CRM platform. Its new **Field Intelligence** module adds AI reception capabilities. **Operator AI** answers calls like a human, captures leads and books them into your calendar, ensuring 100 % call capture. **Chat AI** sits on your website and chats with visitors, converting them into booked jobs while matching your brand voice. Outside of AI, FieldPulse includes scheduling, invoicing and customer management.

## Category Fit

Electricians who rely on incoming phone calls and web enquiries can benefit from FieldPulse’s AI reception. Operator AI ensures that calls are never missed and bookings happen automatically, which is valuable when you’re up a ladder. Chat AI can turn late‑night website visits into appointments. However, the system’s US focus and custom pricing may limit adoption in the UK.

## Key Features

- **Operator AI** – 24/7 phone receptionist that captures calls and books jobs with natural conversation.
- **Chat AI** – website chat assistant that converts visitors into booked jobs and aligns responses with your brand.
- **Job management & CRM** – scheduling, quotes, invoices and customer records.

## Workflow Example

1. A prospect calls your business after hours; Operator AI answers, gathers the job details and books an appointment in your calendar.
2. A visitor browses your website; Chat AI engages them, answers questions and schedules a service.
3. You complete the job and use FieldPulse’s CRM to generate an invoice and record payment.

## Best For

- Electricians seeking to ensure 100 % call capture and automate bookings without hiring a receptionist.

## Not Ideal For

- Contractors needing fixed, transparent pricing.
- UK electricians requiring local forms and compliance features.

## Final Recommendation

FieldPulse’s Field Intelligence brings AI reception and chat capabilities that can significantly improve lead capture for electricians. However, the lack of public pricing and potential US‑centric features mean you should request a demo and confirm UK compatibility before committing.
