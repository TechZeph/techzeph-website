## Quick Verdict

Jobber is a leading field‑service management platform and has recently introduced **Jobber AI**. Its AI helps price jobs accurately, flag upsell opportunities and draft quotes, and it can rewrite client messages or emails in a professional tone. However, the advanced AI features are only available on higher tiers.

## What This Tool Does

Jobber centralises quoting, scheduling, dispatch, invoicing and customer management. **Jobber AI** builds on this by analysing job data to suggest appropriate pricing, flag opportunities for add‑ons and draft quotes. The AI can also rewrite customer emails and messages to maintain a consistent, professional tone and even provide business advice via voice commands. This means you spend less time crafting communications and more time on the tools.

## Category Fit

For electricians who regularly produce quotes and invoices, Jobber’s combination of CRM and AI is a good fit. The AI assistance speeds up quoting and improves professionalism, while the core system handles scheduling, dispatch and payment processing. Jobber’s UK support includes VAT invoicing and integration with local payment gateways, making it suitable for small and medium‑sized electrical businesses.

## Key Features

- **AI‑assisted quoting** – suggests prices, identifies upsell opportunities and drafts quotes.
- **Message rewriting** – rewrites client emails and messages to match your brand tone.
- **Voice commands & business advice** – allows you to use voice to manage jobs and receive business tips.

## Pricing (UK)

| Plan | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Core | ≈£31 per user/month | Starts at $39 per month when billed monthly, or $28/month when billed annually; includes one user. |
| Connect | ≈£93 per month | $119/month or $72/month annually; supports up to five users and adds automation features. |
| Grow | ≈£155 per month | $199/month or $120/month annually; designed for larger teams with advanced features. |

## Pros

- AI helps create accurate quotes, flag upsell opportunities and rewrite messages.
- All‑in‑one CRM handles scheduling, dispatch, invoicing and payments.
- 14‑day free trial allows you to test features before committing.

## Cons

- AI features are available only on higher‑tier plans; Core plan lacks advanced automations.
- Pricing is per user, so costs can increase quickly for larger teams.
- Some users find the price high relative to features.

## Workflow Example

1. Create a new job in Jobber and let Jobber AI suggest pricing and potential add‑ons based on similar work.
2. Generate a quote using the AI‑drafted text; review and send it to the client via email.
3. Once the job is complete, use Jobber to convert the quote into an invoice and accept payment, with the AI rewriting your follow‑up message if needed.

## Best For

- Electricians and small trade businesses needing robust CRM with AI‑assisted quoting.

## Not Ideal For

- Very small teams who want free or ultra‑low‑cost software; Jobber’s per‑user pricing may be too steep for occasional use.
- Contractors looking for AI features like plan take‑offs or voice answering; Jobber AI focuses on quoting and messaging.

## Final Recommendation

Jobber is a polished platform that streamlines quoting, scheduling and invoicing for UK electricians. The addition of Jobber AI brings helpful automation, saving time on pricing and communications. Evaluate whether the higher‑tier plans, which unlock the full AI capabilities, fit your budget and needs.
