## What to look for first

For most UK electricians, the biggest win is turning site notes into clear quotes faster.  
Prioritise tools that can:

- convert rough notes into structured quote drafts
- reuse your standard line items and exclusions
- export cleanly to PDF or your existing invoicing stack

## Red flags before you buy

- no way to lock default labour rates and markups
- poor UK spelling and terminology in client-facing copy
- no audit trail on who changed totals and when

## Recommended workflow

1. Capture site notes on mobile.
2. Use AI to create first-draft quote text.
3. Manually check labour, materials, and contingency.
4. Send with clear assumptions and validity window.

AI should draft, not approve pricing.
