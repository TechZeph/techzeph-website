## Quick Verdict

Workiz stands out for its Genius AI Answering, which acts as a 24/7 receptionist by converting missed calls into booked jobs. The company claims it converts 7 out of 10 leads into scheduled work and saves up to 35 % of admin time. However, the pricing is relatively high and the AI focuses narrowly on call handling rather than quoting or estimating.

## What This Tool Does

Workiz is a field service management platform covering scheduling, dispatching, invoicing and customer communication. Its Genius AI Answering tool answers calls, texts and web chats on your behalf, books services directly into your calendar and responds to common queries. The AI claims a 70 % conversion rate, turning seven out of ten leads into booked jobs and ensuring zero missed opportunities. It can also let clients reschedule themselves, freeing up about 35 % of administrative time.

## Category Fit

Electricians often rely on phone leads and may miss calls while on site. Workiz’s AI receptionist ensures you capture every enquiry and book jobs without hiring extra staff. For businesses with a high volume of service calls or emergency work, this feature can have a direct impact on revenue. However, for electricians who primarily quote from plans or rely on written estimates, the AI may not add significant value.

## Key Features

- **Genius AI Answering** – automatically answers calls, texts and web chats and books jobs, converting around 70 % of leads into appointments.
- **Self‑serve scheduling** – customers can schedule or reschedule their own appointments, saving up to 35 % of admin time.
- **Dispatch and invoicing** – manage jobs, assign technicians and send invoices within the same system.

## Pros

- AI Answering converts missed calls into jobs and operates around the clock.
- Allows clients to self‑schedule, saving significant admin time.
- Integrates scheduling, dispatch and invoicing in one platform.

## Cons

- Pricing is relatively high compared with simple scheduling tools.
- AI features focus on call answering rather than quoting, estimating or document generation.
- May be overkill for electricians with low call volumes or those relying on written bids.

## Workflow Example

1. A potential client calls or texts your business while you’re on site; Genius AI Answering picks up, answers questions and books an appointment.
2. The appointment appears in your Workiz calendar; you can assign it to yourself or a team member.
3. After the job, generate an invoice through Workiz and follow up using the built‑in communication tools.

## Best For

- Electricians with high inbound call volume who want to ensure no lead is missed.

## Not Ideal For

- Contractors who primarily quote via plans and need AI for estimating or takeoffs.
- Solo electricians with few calls; a simpler, cheaper tool might suffice.

## Final Recommendation

Workiz’s Genius AI Answering is a powerful tool for UK electricians who rely on phone leads and want an always‑on receptionist. It can significantly increase booking rates and reduce admin time. However, the cost and the narrow focus on call handling mean it’s best for high‑volume service businesses. If your primary need is estimating or quoting rather than managing calls, consider other AI tools better suited to those tasks.
