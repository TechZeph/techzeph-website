## Quick Verdict

Powered Now combines job management with simple AI‑powered text tools. Its AI can proof‑read existing quotes, invoices and messages and even generate new wording that matches your tone.

## What This Tool Does

Powered Now is a cloud‑based job management system designed for UK tradespeople. It lets you create and send quotes, invoices and certificates from a mobile device or desktop. The AI tools launched in 2025 focus on content generation: they proof‑read and improve your quotes or emails and can produce brand‑new text that matches your style. Everything syncs across devices, so you can manage jobs, communicate with customers and record expenses on site or in the office.

## Category Fit

For electricians, managing paperwork, certificates and communication is as important as installing circuits. Powered Now is tailored to UK compliance with NICEIC certificates and digital forms. Its AI helps you polish technical descriptions and client emails without spending extra time, making it ideal for small electrical firms that want to look professional and stay compliant without a full back‑office team.

## Key Features

- **AI‑driven text assistant** – proofreads and rewrites your quotes, invoices and messages, or generates new text to match your tone.
- **All‑in‑one job management** – create quotes, schedule jobs, track expenses and produce digital forms such as electrical installation certificates.
- **Offline capability** – access and edit jobs even when you’re off‑grid; syncs when back online.

## Pros

- Affordable monthly pricing with a free trial.
- AI assistant improves the quality of quotes and emails without extra effort.
- Built for UK trades: supports electrical certificates and VAT invoices.

## Cons

- AI features focus on text rewriting rather than deeper automation.
- Lacks advanced scheduling and job‑costing analytics found in larger platforms.

## Workflow Example

1. Capture a new enquiry from a client and create a job in Powered Now.
2. Draft a detailed quote using your normal wording, then use the AI assistant to proof‑read and refine the description before sending it.
3. Once the job is completed, generate an invoice and send it via email; the AI can rewrite the message to sound professional and consistent with your branding.

## Best For

- Solo electricians or small teams who want an affordable, all‑in‑one job management tool.
- Tradespeople who need help polishing written communication and staying compliant with UK certification.

## Not Ideal For

- Large electrical contractors requiring advanced scheduling, inventory management or AI‑driven quoting.

## Final Recommendation

Powered Now is a solid entry‑level choice for UK electricians who want to digitise their paperwork and improve their customer communications. Its AI writing assistant helps you produce professional‑sounding quotes and invoices without becoming a copywriter, and the low price makes it accessible to sole traders.
