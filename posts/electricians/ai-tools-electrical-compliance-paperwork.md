## Paperwork tasks worth automating

For compliance workflows, AI can assist with:

- drafting method statements from your templates
- converting engineer notes into structured summaries
- creating checklists before final submission

## Do not outsource responsibility

AI output should always be reviewed against current UK requirements and scheme guidance.  
Treat AI as a drafting assistant, not a compliance authority.

## Low-risk adoption pattern

1. Start with internal drafts only.
2. Compare AI output with your current process for 2-4 weeks.
3. Keep a reviewer checklist for every document set.

This keeps quality high while reducing repetitive admin.
