## Where AI helps most

AI job-management support is strongest in communication tasks:

- summarising WhatsApp and email threads
- generating next-day schedules from open jobs
- writing concise progress updates for clients

## Keep decisions human

Use AI for drafting and summarising, but keep these manual:

- final resource allocation
- safety-critical sequencing
- sign-off messages and contractual wording

## A simple setup

1. Keep one source of truth for jobs.
2. Feed only the required context into AI.
3. Review outputs before sending to clients.

This gives speed gains without losing control.
